About AbilityAssist

The objective of AbilityAssist is to provide comprehensive assistance to individuals living with visual impairment at Tshwane University of Technology South Campus. We aim to empower users by employing cutting-edge sound detection technology, obstacle identification features, and voice-over assistance to guide them seamlessly throughout the campus. Additionally, AbilityAssist calculates distances for accurate arrival times to destinations, ensuring efficient navigation for users.

We believe that everyone, regardless of their abilities, should have equal access to opportunities and resources. That's why we work tirelessly to develop innovative solutions that improve the lives of people with disabilities.

With a team of dedicated professionals and experts in accessibility, we strive to create a more inclusive society where everyone can thrive and reach their full potential.
